Welcome to Lincoln Suites!

A luxury experience awaits you at Lincoln Suites! This hotel in small-town Marshall, Ill. is closely located near restaurants and shops and offers the comforts of home. Our prime location at I-70 and Rt. 1 makes Lincoln Suites accessible for large events such as Marshall Autumn Festival, Covered Bridge Festival, Scheid Diesel Extravaganza, area Homecomings, and others in the Wabash Valley area. Since 2011, Lincoln Suites has provided a high-quality and personal service at the best price we know our guests have come to expect and enjoy!
